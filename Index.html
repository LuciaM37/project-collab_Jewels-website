About Nonsisa'

Nonsisa' comes from the Italian "non si sa" that means "It is not known".

My jewels are all hand made with love and inspired by my "double identity". I am half Greek and half Italian and I have always lived between these two countries.

Each piece contains a piece of my memories: the colours of my summers in southern Italy when I was a child, my experience in Bologna, my voyages in Spain, the everyday discovery of my city Athens...

Come with me to discover all of them!
